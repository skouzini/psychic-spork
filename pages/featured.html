A Cappella Club

The BYU A Cappella Club was founded in Fall of 1992. It has continued since that time to be the center of the a cappella community at the university. Each semester the Club hosts the A Cappella Jam and other events that showcase current groups on campus.

Quark: Sci-fi/Fantasy

Quark is BYU's official Science-Fiction and Fantasy Club; although honestly, we're just into all things geek. Aside from occasional socials, we don't hold meetings like other clubs; rather, because our members are interested in so many areas of geekdom, we offer a variety of regular or semi-regular activities catering to these differing interests. Attend one or ATTEND THEM ALL!
